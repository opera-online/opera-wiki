## What is Opera?

Opera is a dynamic and ever-evolving community-driven organization that acts as a web of resources for individuals who are passionate about shaping the future of the art industry. Our goal is to democratize the industry by promoting transparency and speaking about art plainly. At Opera, we believe in expanding the boundaries of the art industry through symbiotic relationships. Above all, it is an evolving experimenton art patronage.
